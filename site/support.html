Common questions

How do I install HideZero?

Install directly from the Google Workspace Marketplace. Once installed, open any Google Sheet and find HideZero under the Extensions menu.

Where do I get a license key?

Purchase a license at hidezero.gumroad.com. Gumroad sends your license key by email immediately after purchase.

I lost my license key.

Check your original Gumroad purchase email. If you can't find it, visit app.gumroad.com/library and log in with the email address you used to purchase — your key is listed there.

The add-on isn't appearing in the Extensions menu.

Try refreshing the page. If it still doesn't appear, go to Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons and confirm HideZero is listed and enabled.

How do I update or clear my license key?

Go to Extensions → HideZero → Manage License Key. From there you can enter a new key or clear the existing one.

Will hiding zeros affect my formulas?

No. HideZero only changes the cell's display format — the underlying value is never touched. Formulas referencing those cells continue to work exactly as before.
