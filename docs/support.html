Common questions

How do I activate HideZero?

After installing from the Google Workspace Marketplace, open a spreadsheet and go to Extensions → HideZero. You'll be prompted to enter your Gumroad license key. Purchase a key at hidezero.gumroad.com.

Where is my license key?

Your license key is emailed to you by Gumroad after purchase. You can also find it in your Gumroad Library. If you've lost it, contact Gumroad support or email us and we'll help you locate it.

The add-on says my license is invalid

Make sure you entered the key exactly as provided (no extra spaces). If the issue persists, email us with your Gumroad order ID and we'll look into it.

How do I clear my license key?

Go to Extensions → HideZero → Manage License Key and choose the option to clear it. This removes the key from the current spreadsheet only.

Will hiding zeros affect my formulas or data?

No. HideZero only changes the number format of cells — it never modifies your data or formulas. Your cell values remain exactly as they are.
