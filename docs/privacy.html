Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

HideZero ("the add-on") is a Google Sheets™ add-on developed by Avi Klayman. This policy explains what data the add-on collects and how it is used.

Data collected

The add-on stores a single piece of data: the Gumroad license key you enter when first activating the add-on. This key is saved in the spreadsheet's DocumentProperties (a Google-managed storage local to the spreadsheet) and is used solely to verify your license on each use.

The add-on does not collect, store, or transmit your name, email address, spreadsheet contents, or any other personal data.

License verification

When you run the add-on, it sends your license key to a Google Apps Script Web App operated by the developer. That endpoint verifies the key against the Gumroad License API and returns only a valid/invalid result. No spreadsheet data is included in this request.

Third-party services

License purchases are handled by Gumroad. Please refer to Gumroad's privacy policy for information on how they handle purchase data.

Data retention

Your license key is stored in DocumentProperties until you clear it via "Manage License Key" in the add-on menu, or until the spreadsheet is deleted. The developer does not retain a copy of your license key.
